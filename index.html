🤖 Ollama Setup
CharacterVerse runs AI entirely on your PC using Ollama. No API keys, no accounts, no internet needed for chat. Completely free and private.
  1. Download & install ollama.com (free, Windows/Mac/Linux)
  2. Double-click start.bat in your e:\personal_Ai folder
  3. The site opens at localhost:8080 — Ollama handles everything else automatically

After picking, open a terminal and run: ollama pull <model-name>
